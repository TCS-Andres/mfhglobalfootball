The Problem

The path to pro is confusing, and not every agent earns your trust.

Talented players and families too often navigate this alone, or alongside someone who puts the commission before the player. We think families deserve better.

Unlicensed agents

Operators working without structure or accountability, outside FIFA's rules.

Confusing regulations

Work permits, quotas, transfer windows, and minor rules that decide a career, often misunderstood.

Language barriers

Legal complexity is hard enough. It is harder when the contract is not in your first language.

Approaches to minors

Scouts and intermediaries reaching young players without the parents in the room.

Your Guide
